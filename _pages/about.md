I am currently an Assistant Professor of Economics and Wieler Family Assistant Professor in the Morrissey College of Arts and Sciences at [Boston College](https://www.bc.edu/bc-web/schools/mcas/departments/economics.html/) in Chestnut Hill, MA, USA. My research interests are in industrial organization and antitrust. In particular, I study how automobile retail markets are organized and the implications of regulations and market power of dealers and manufacturers. I also have interests in issues of market power and antitrust issues in airline markets.
